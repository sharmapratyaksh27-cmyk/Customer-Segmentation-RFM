**Project Title**: Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis.

**Objective**: To group e-commerce customers into distinct segments based on purchasing behavior to enable targeted marketing.

**Tools Used**: Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Seaborn.

**Key Insights**: 
1. Cluster 0: "Champions" (High Value & Loyal): Recent buyers (avg. 27 days), frequent shoppers (19x), and highest spenders ($11K). Action: Reward with VIP perks and exclusive loyalty programs to maximize retention.

2. Cluster 1: "Lost Customers" (Churned): Inactive for over a year (avg. 396 days) with the lowest spend ($325) and frequency (1.3x). Action: Use low-cost, automated re-engagement emails. Avoid heavy marketing spend here.

3. Cluster 2: "At-Risk" (Declining Engagement): Solid past spend ($2K) and frequency (5x), but inactive for a long time (avg. 228 days). Action: Deploy aggressive win-back campaigns and personalized discounts to prevent total churn.

4. Cluster 3: "Recent & Promising" (Newer/Low Spend): Bought very recently (avg. 28 days) but with low frequency (3x) and spend ($865). Action: Nurture into "Champions" through targeted upselling and product recommendations.
